Delivery Information

You can change your delivery preferences in Your Orders at any time after placing your order as long as the order hasn't entered the shipping process yet. For all deliveries, you consent to your order being left in a safe place.

  • Group My Items Into As Few Deliveries As Possible Your order will be grouped into the fewest number of deliveries possible. This may mean that it will take longer to be shipped, depending on availability, but there'll only be one per-delivery charge. Please be aware that items whose availability is listed as 'Not Yet Released' or 'Back Ordered' can potentially delay deliveries with this option.
  • I want my items sooner. Ship them as soon as they become available (at additional cost) Your items are shipped as they become available, beginning with those already in stock. You should choose this option if you want to receive your order quickly and the delivery may be delayed by the availability of a single item, for example. However, we'll charge you for the delivery of each item at the rates quoted to you at the time you placed your order.

The number of deliveries we make for your order depends on: Our fulfilment network If your order has unrelated items such as a book, a computer and a cordless drill, they may be shipped separately, depending on where in our fulfilment network they're located. Note: You won't be charged more for delivery than was originally quoted to you at the time you placed your order.

Click and Collect Information

Where do I pick up my Click & Collect order?

Pick up your Click & Collect order from the Service Counter, usually located at the rear of the store. Our friendly team members can direct you to the right place.

What time can I collect my order from store?

Please wait for your confirmation email or SMS prior to visiting the store – normally sent within 24 hours of placing your order.

You can check store opening hours in the checkout when you select a store – just select ‘Store Details’. You can also check store details at any time using the Kmart Store Locator on our website.

What do I need to bring to collect my Click & Collect order?

For Click & Collect, make sure you bring either

  • Your photo ID
  • Your order collection notification (on your phone is fine)

Can I get my Click & Collect order delivered?

You can get your Click & Collect order delivered using GetItFast. Get It Fast is a third party delivery service that Kmart has partnered with, to offer delivery to Click & Collect customers at select stores only within 24 hours.
